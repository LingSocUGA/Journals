\section{Interview Module}
  \begin{enumerate}
    \subsection{Demographics}
    \item Dans quelle année que t'as été né?\\
      \longtrans{In what year were you born?}
    \item Éoù tu restes?\\
      \longtrans{Where do you live?}
    \begin{enumerate}
      \item Ça c'est éoù t'as grandi, aussit?\\
        \longtrans{Is that where you grew up, too?}
      \item Ta famille reste dans le même village que toi?\\
        \longtrans{Does your family live in the same town as you?}
    \end{enumerate}
    \item Tu fais quoi comme métier?\\
      \longtrans{What is your profession?}
    \item Quoi t'as fait après finir l'école/le high school?\\
      \longtrans{What did you do after finishing (high) school?}
    % \item T'as été au collège? Quel collège?\\
    %   \longtrans{Did you go to college? Which college?}
    \item Comment t'as appris le français? C'est ta première langue?\\
      \longtrans{How did you learn French? Is it your first language?}\\
      (Ask near the end of the interview.)
    \item Quoi c'est ton sexe?\\
      \longtrans{What is your gender?}\\
      (Ask near the end of the interview if they have not gendered themselves through adjective use.)
\subsection{Casual Conversation}
  \subsubsection{3rd person plural}
    \item Quelles qualités de jobs le monde fait dans ton village?\\
      \longtrans{What kind of work do the people in your town do?}
    \item Comment t'as rencontré les amis que t'as aujourd'hui?\\
      \longtrans{How did you meet the friends you have today?}
    % \item Quoi tu penses pour le monde dans ton village? Le monde se traite bien? T'as un exemple?\\
    %   \longtrans{What are the people in your town like? People treat each other well? Do you have an example?}
  \subsubsection{3rd person singular}
    \item Y'a combien de personnes dans ta famille?\\
      \longtrans{How many people are there in your family?}
    \item Ton/ta <family member> est comment? T'as une histoire de <family member>?\\
      \longtrans{What is <family member> like? Do you have a story about <family member>?}
  \subsubsection{1st person plural}
    \item T'as pris des vacances avec ta famille ou peut-être d'autre monde? Quoi c'était des vacances mémorables? \\
      \longtrans{Have you taken vacations with your family or perhaps other people? What was a memorable vacation?}
    \item Quelle activité t'aimes faire avec le monde? \\
      \longtrans{What activity do you like to do with people?}
\subsection{Race and Ethnicity}
    \item Quoi c'est un Cadien, d'après toi? \\
      \longtrans{What is a Cajun, in your opinion?}
    \item Quoi c'est un Creole, d'après toi? \\
      \longtrans{What is a Creole, in your opinion?}
    \item Quelle qualité de relation les Cadiens ont avec les Créoles? \\
      \longtrans{What kind of relationship do Cajuns have with Creoles?}
    % \item Tous les Cadiens et Créoles sont américains, aussit? \\
    %   \longtrans{Are all Cajuns and Creoles also Americans?}
    % \item Y'a du monde de différent de races dans ton village? \\
    %   \longtrans{Are there people of different races in your town?}
    % \item C'est vrai que le monde pense que tous les Cadiens sont d'une race et puis tous les Créoles d'une autre race ou c'est plus compliqué? \\
    %   \longtrans{Is it true that people think that all Cajuns are one race and all Creoles are another race or is it more complicated?}
    \item Si t'aurais besoin de déclarer ta race, quoi tu dirais? \\
      \longtrans{If you had to name your race, what would you say?}
    \item Les Cadiens et les Créoles parlent français de la même manière? \\
      \longtrans{Do Cajuns and Creoles speak French the same way?}
    % \item Quoi tu penses pour la langue française? \\
    %   \longtrans{What do you think about the French language?}
    % \item Le français est toujours important dans la Louisiane? Combien de monde parle français au jour d'aujourd'hui? \\
    %   \longtrans{Is French still important in Louisiana? How many people speak French nowadays?}
    % \item Tu veux qu'on continue à parler français à l'avenir? \\
%   \longtrans{Do you want French to continue being spoken in the future?}
\subsection{Wrap-Up (after all sections have been completed)}
  \subsubsection{2nd person singular}
    \item T'as des questions pour moi?\\
      \longtrans{Do you have any questions for me?}
  \end{enumerate}

\section{Social Network Data}
  \begin{enumerate}
  \subsection{Name Generators}
    \item Tous les temps en temps, le monde parle pour les affaires importantes avec d'autre monde. Si tu peux jongler pour les six derniers mois, qui sont les individus avec qui toi tu parles pour les affaires qui te sont importantes? Donne-moi rien que leur prénom ou leurs initiales.\\
      \longtrans{From time to time, most people discuss important matters with other people. Looking back over the last six months, who are the people with whom you discussed matters important to you? Just tell me their first names or initials.}
    \item Qui sont les personnes avec qui tu parles souvent français? Donne-moi rien que leur prénom ou leurs initiales.\\
      \longtrans{Who are the people with whoom you often speak French? Just tell me their first names or initials.}
  \subsection{Name Interpretors}
    \item Pour chaque personne sur cette liste, quoi c'est ton rapport avec lui? La personne est un(e) ami(e) ou un parent ou autre chose?\\
      \longtrans{For each person on this list, what is your relationship with them? Is the person a friend or a relative or something else?}
    \item Quelles personnes sur cette liste sont des personnes avec qui tu parles souvent français?\\
      \longtrans{Which people on this list are people with whom you often speak French?}
    \item Pour chaque personne sur cette liste, quoi c'est son éthnicité, c'est-à-dire son \foreign{ethnicity}?\\
      \longtrans{For each person on this list, what is their ethnicity, that is to say their ethnicity?}
    \item Pour chaque personne sur cette liste, quoi c'est sa race?\\
      \longtrans{For each person on this list, what is their race?}
  \end{enumerate}

\section{Translation Task}
  \begin{enumerate}
    \item[] \setting{Comment tu dirais d'habitude les phrases suivantes, personnel?}\\
      \longtrans{How would you usually say the following phrases, personally?}
    \item[] \setting{T'après souper à un restaurant de haute qualité avec ton père. Ta nièce et ton neveu, qui sont des enfants, sont égal avec toi.}\\
      \longtrans{You are having dinner at a very nice restaurant with your father. Your niece and nephew, who are children, are also with you.}
    \item[] \setting{Un serveur arrive à la table et dit:}\\
      \longtrans{A waiter arrives at the table and says:}
    \item (2pl.MFV) How are you all doing this evening?
    \item[] \setting{Tu réponds:}\\
      \longtrans{You respond:}
    \item (expl) It's raining outside.
    \item (1pl.MF) So we're good but a little wet.
    \item[] \setting{Le serveur répond:}\\
      \longtrans{The waiter responds:}
    \item (2sg.MV) Perhaps you would like a coffee?
    \item[] \setting{Tu dis:}\\
      \longtrans{You say:}
    \item Is it hot already?
    \item[] \setting{Le serveur dit:}\\
      \longtrans{The waiter says:}
    \item Of course, and what will the others have?
    \item[] \setting{Tu dis:}\\
      \longtrans{You say:}
    \item (3sg.AM) My father, he will also have a coffee.
    \item (3pl.AMF) My niece and nephew, they will have apple juice.
    \item[] \setting{Le serveur demande:}\\
      \longtrans{The waitor asks:}
    \item (2pl.MFV) And would you all like some appetizers to start?
    \item[] \setting{Tu réponds:}\\
      \longtrans{You respond:}
    \item (1pl.M?MF?) My father and I, we'll have some gratons.
    \item (1sg, 3pl.IM) I hear they are the best in town.
    \item[] \setting{Les enfants disent à ton père:}\\
      \longtrans{The children say to your father:}
    \item (2sg.MV) Can you order some potatoes for us?
    \item[] \setting{Tu réponds:}\\
      \longtrans{You respond:}
    \item (3pl.IF) Potatoes? They aren't served here.
    \item (2pl.MFT) You all can have some shrimp.
    \item[] \setting{Le serveur part, et tu demandes à ta nièce:}\\
      \longtrans{The waiter leaves, and you ask your niece:}
    \item (2sg.FT) Have you been having fun at your dance class?
    \item (3pl.AF) The other girls there, are they good dancers?
    \item[] \setting{Ta nièce répond:}\\
      \longtrans{Your niece responds:}
    \item (3sg.AF) Some are. My best friend, she's the best dancer.
    \item[] \setting{Tu demandes à ton père:}\\
      \longtrans{You ask your father:}
    \item (2sg.MF) Have you been fishing with your friend Jon lately?
    \item[] \setting{Ton père répond:}\\
      \longtrans{Your father responds:}
    \item (1pl.M) Yes. In fact, we just bought a boat to use.
    \item[] \setting{Tu demandes:}\\
      \longtrans{You ask:}
    \item (3sg.IM) Is it a brand new boat?
    \item[] \setting{Ton père dit:}\\
      \longtrans{Your father says:}
    \item (3sg.IM) Oh yes, it's brand new.
    \item (1sg.M) I bought a new fishing rod, too.
    \item (3sg.IF, 1pl.M) It's perfect for the kind of water we fish in.
    \item (expl) It will be necessary to schedule a time with Jon for our next trip.
    \item[] \setting{Tu demandes:}\\
      \longtrans{You ask:}
    \item How do fishermen know where to go to find fish?
    \item[] \setting{Ton père réponds:}\\
      \longtrans{Your father answers:}
    \item (imp) You learn a lot of tricks.
    \item (imp) You have to fish a lot to figure them out.
    \item[] \setting{Ton père à ton neveu:}\\
      \longtrans{You father to your nephew:}
    \item (2sg.MT) Would you like to go fishing with me?
    \item[] \setting{Ta mère arrive et s'excuse d'être en retard. Toi, tu dis:}\\
      \longtrans{Your mother arrives and apologizes for being late. You say:}
    \item (imp) It's perfectly alright.
    \item (2sg.FV) You aren't too late.
    \item[] \setting{Le serveur revient pour prendre vos commandes.}\\
      \longtrans{The waiter returns to take your orders.}
  \end{enumerate}
